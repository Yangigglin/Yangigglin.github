Soup

A seafood congee is a nice soup consisting of shrimp, rice, green
onion and salt. It is unbelievably delicous and easy to cook.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Shrimp
  • Green Onion
  • Salt

Directions:

  1. Cook a regular rice soup
  2. When the rice soup is cooked, add shrimp to the soup
  3. Cook the soup until the shrimp is cooked
  4. Add proper amount of salt
  5. Add green onion, stir a few times
  6. The delicous rice soup is ready to serve
